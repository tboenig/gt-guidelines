General Information

About this task

From a structural point of view, a book consists of different page types and the pages contain different regions with different structures and content. In particular, the TextRegion can be specified in more detail as a column title, heading, paragraph region, and so on... Within these regions, the individual texts can show typographical features that can also be labelled..

Depending on the level of capture (Structure GT Level), layout and structure analysis should generally include the following:

Procedure

  1. Marking of the page types
  2. Marking of the page margin as well as looking at the print space.
  3. Marking of the page regions
  4. Marking of particular regions on a page
  5. Marking of typographical peculiarities
  6. Marking of reading order
Published by OCR-D.
